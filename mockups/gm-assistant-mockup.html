Strahd's weaknesses

Campaign: Curse of Strahd
DM
What are Strahd's known weaknesses, and how can the party exploit them in the final confrontation at Castle Ravenloft?
AI
Based on your campaign documents, Strahd has several key weaknesses the party can exploit:

1. The Sunsword — A sentient weapon that radiates sunlight. Strahd takes radiant damage and has disadvantage on attack rolls while in its light. Your notes indicate the party found the blade in the Amber Temple (Session 9).

2. The Holy Symbol of Ravenkind — Can paralyze Strahd for up to 1 minute (DC 15 Charisma save). Per your session notes, Ireena currently holds this item.

3. Sunlight Sensitivity — Strahd has disadvantage on attack rolls and Perception checks in direct sunlight. The Heart of Sorrow protects him from some radiant damage, but destroying it first removes that shield.

4. Running Water — Strahd takes 20 acid damage if he ends his turn in running water. Castle Ravenloft has limited water features, but creative use of spells could exploit this.

5. Wooden Stake — If driven into his heart while incapacitated in his coffin, Strahd is paralyzed until the stake is removed.
